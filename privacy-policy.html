PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: October 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy for DVE Doctor’s Billing Services LLC ("we," "us," or "our") describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you visit our website at www.dvebilling.com or contact us about our construction and design services.
‍
Questions or concerns? Reading this Privacy Policy will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

SUMMARY OF KEY POINTS

What personal information do we process? When you visit or use our Services, we may process personal information depending on how you interact with us, such as when you contact us about a project or request information about our services.

Do we process any sensitive personal information? We do not process sensitive personal information.

Do we collect any information from third parties? We may collect limited information from public sources or business referrals, but we do not purchase data from third-party marketing companies.

How do we process your information? We process your information to communicate with you about potential projects, provide our construction and design services, and improve our Services.

In what situations do we share personal information? We may share information with contractors, subcontractors, and suppliers as necessary to complete projects, and in limited other circumstances described below.

What are your rights? Depending on where you are located, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information.

How do you exercise your rights? The easiest way is by contacting us directly using the contact information provided on our website.

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you provide to us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

• Contact us through our website contact form
• Email us about potential projects
• Call us to discuss construction or design services
• Provide information during project consultations

This information may include:

• Name and contact information (email, phone, address)
• Project details and requirements
• Property information for potential projects
• Preferences and specifications for construction or design work

Information automatically collected

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information, including:

• Internet Protocol (IP) address
• Browser and device characteristics
• Operating system and language preferences
• Pages you visit on our site
• Time and date of your visit
• Referring website

This information does not reveal your specific identity but helps us understand how visitors use our website and improve our Services.

‍

2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

We process your personal information for the following purposes:Our intellectual property

• To respond to your inquiries and provide information about our services
• To communicate with you about potential or ongoing projects
• To provide construction and design services including project planning, coordination, and execution
• To maintain business records related to projects and client relationships
• To improve our Services and understand how our website is used
• To comply with legal obligations related to our business operations‍

3. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE
YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We may share your personal information in the following situations:

Project-related sharing:

• Contractors and subcontractors working on your project
• Suppliers and vendors providing materials or services
• Architects and engineers collaborating on design work
• Permit offices and inspectors as required by law

Business purposes:

• Service providers who help us operate our website or business (such as web hosting)
• Legal compliance when required by law or to protect our rights

Business transfers:

• In connection with any merger, sale of assets, or acquisition of our business
‍
We require all third parties to respect the security of your personal information and treat it in accordance with the law.‍

4. DO WE USE COOKIES AND OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES?

We may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information when you interact with our Services. These technologies help us:

• Remember your preferences
• Understand how you use our website
• Improve website performance and security

Most web browsers accept cookies by default, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

5. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

We keep your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, including:

• Active project information for the duration of the project and up to 7 years after completion for business and legal purposes
• General inquiries to follow up on potential future projects
• Website analytics data to improve our Services

When we no longer need your personal information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

6. DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION FROM MINORS?

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age. Our Services are intended for adults seeking construction and design services. If you are under 18, please do not provide any personal information through our Services.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a minor, we will promptly delete such information. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

7. WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS?

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

• Access - Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
• Correction - Request that we correct any inaccurate personal information
• Restriction - Request that we limit how we use your personal information
• Objection - Object to our processing of your personal information
• Data portability - Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

Withdrawing consent: If we are processing your information based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time by contacting us.

8. DO WE MAKE UPDATES TO THIS POLICY?

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

If we make material changes, we may notify you by prominently posting a notice on our website or by directly contacting you. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. HOW CAN YOU CONTACT US ABOUT THIS POLICY?

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through:

• Website contact form: www.dvebilling.com
By contacting us directly using the contact information provided on our website

We will do our best to respond to your privacy-related inquiries promptly and thoroughly.
